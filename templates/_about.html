For Prospective Students (Prefrosh)

Ultimate can be one of the best experiences you'll have at Brown, and we're always excited to welcome new students to the program.

Interested in playing ultimate at Brown? Let us know when you'll be on campus, and we'll arrange for you to meet some of the team and come to one of our practices. We're also happy to answer any of your questions over email, whether about ultimate or about life at Brown in general. You can email either one of the captains from the contact information above.

Brownian Motion has all types on its team. Short guys to taller ones. Former soccer players to basketball players to track athletes. Guys who never played ultimate before college to guys who played in a summer league or played at their high school. Come in the fall in shape and show dedication and we'll take care of the rest. Brown has three different ultimate teams with different levels of commitment: the A team (Brownian Motion), B team (P Funk), and C team (Mothership). Each year we have an instructional, relaxed fall league for all new and returning members of the program, before cuts for the teams are made. It gives everyone a chance to show their stuff and pick up some new skills. Anyone can come to fall league, so look for announcements about playing ultimate in early September.

Each year at ADOCH, there is a special late-night ultimate frisbee event, and we would love to see you there! Current team members put on the event, so come out to play with us and ask us anything. Also at ADOCH, during the Extracurricular Fair, there is a table for ultimate frisbee, and this is another great way to put your name on a mailing list and hear more about playing ultimate at Brown.
